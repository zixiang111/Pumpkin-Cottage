About Us

Pumpkin Cottage Cattery is located in Essen, North Rhine-Westphalia, registered with Felidae e.V.
Breeding focus: Golden shaded, Blue golden shaded, Lilac golden and Chocolate golden shaded
Specializing in sweet traditional-type cats with full muzzle

We adhere to scientific breeding principles with only one litter per year. Meticulous feeding ensures each kitten receives individual attention.
Comprehensive socialization training develops kittens' friendly and affectionate personalities.

  • Complete genetic and hereditary disease testing for breeding cats
  • Annual comprehensive health check-ups
  • Comprehensive after-sales guarantee
